How does eCoinSupply wallet work?

eCoinSupply wallet features seven different accounts: Euro, Pound Sterling, US Dollar, Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum and XRP. The idea behind the accounts is that you can store your funds in any of them, transfer between any of the 7 accounts anytime you want and even more. This feature allows you to take precise control over your money by spreading your funds across the seven accounts, making it easy to minimize the risks of market volatility.

eCoinSupply has taken the best features of modern digital banking and integrated them with innovative Bitcoin technology to create a simple interface that makes managing your finances easier than ever.

How do I exchange currencies using eCoinSupply?

eCoinSupply allows you to exchange multiple currencies: Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ripple, Ethereum, US Dollars, Great Britain Pounds, and Euros. An exchange of any currency is considered as a transfer operation, so the system uses internal rates to calculate the amount you receive in the end.

To exchange one currency to another within eCoinSupply, please do the following:

1. Log into your eCoinSupply account;

2. Go to the Accounts section and click "Exchange":

3. Choose the currency and the sum you want to exchange

4. Choose the currency you want to receive.

5. Click the big blue “Exchange” button.

Your funds will be exchanged in up to ten minutes.

Please note: We charge a conversion fee of 1% for each currency exchange transaction. This fee is included in the "total" sum, shown to you by the internal transfer form.

How do I create a new Bitcoin address?

For your convenience, we have made it possible to create several Bitcoin addresses linked to a single wallet within your Bitcoin account.

Your transaction history can become a bit overwhelming and difficult to navigate. What if
you need a particular deposit or a group of deposits shown separately from all the others? For this purpose, you can create a different Bitcoin address so that you can clearly see what amount you were sent in the particular case.

You may want to create a new wallet address for some other reason or even without it.

With eCoinSupply you can do that hassle-free and have different addresses for one wallet!

Here is a small guide on how to create a new address within your eCoinSupply wallet.

You can create a new address for every account (BTC, LTC, XRP, EUR, USD, GBP) you have in your eCoinSupply wallet. On the Accounts page click the name of an account you wish to create a new address for.

In the "Addresses" tab you can see all created Bitcoin addresses, the date of their
creation and a total amount received on each address.

You can create multiple Bitcoin addresses and each address will be active and could be
used to send funds to. Deposited funds will be displayed in the same single wallet on the
Accounts page.

How does the Litecoin account work?

What is Litecoin?

Litecoin (LTC) is a peer-to-peer currency that enables instant payments all over the world. Litecoin is an open source, global payment network that is decentralised and doesn’t have central authorities. Thus you can fully control your own finances. LTC features lower transaction confirmation times and improved storage efficiency compared to other cryptocurrencies.

How to deposit LTC into your eCoinSupply account?

Find your LTC account address on the Accounts tab and click on it: the address will be automatically copied to your clipboard. Alternatively, you can click on your Litecoin account and copy the address manually.

The eCoinSupply LTC addresses come in a new format. If your external wallet does not support new addresses yet, no worries, you can easily convert your eCoinSupply address to the old format to make a deposit.

Please note: The minimum sum to deposit is 0.00000001 LTC. The funds will be shown on the balance of your eCoinSupply LTC account after 3 confirmations of the network.

Important! Please make sure to deposit LTC from external wallets into your eCoinSupply LTC account. In case you send litecoins to your eCoinSupply BTC, ETH, XRP, EUR, USD, or GBP accounts, the funds will NOT be received.

How to convert LTC to other currencies with eCoinSupply?

You can convert LTC to any of the supported currencies: BTC, ETH, XRP, EUR, GBP, and USD. Simply click to "Exchange" button next to your LTC account and choose the currency and the amount you would like to convert.

Please note: The amount you’ll receive will be indicated in a pop-up window after you click "Exchange" but before clicking “Confirm”.

Sending LTC to an external wallet

You can send LTC to either LTC or BTC wallets. Don’t forget to choose whether to send your LTC to a Litecoin or a Bitcoin address. To send funds to an external wallet, click "Withdraw" button next to your LTC account, enter the address, specify the amount, and click “Withdraw”.

For more information regarding LTC withdrawals, please visit our step-by-step guide.

Please note: the minimum withdrawal amount is 0.001 LTC.

Important: if you notice that after pasting the receiving LTC address it is marked as outdated, and the system suggests converting it to the new format, please check this article to proceed.
