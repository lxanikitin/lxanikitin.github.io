Affiliations

 Who can become a eCoinSupply affiliate?

The affiliation program is catered to websites whose content is crytpoasset related, and notably those listing exchange platforms. Websites that promote eCoinSupply receive a commision for every new referred customer.

 How do I become a eCoinSupply affiliate?

You can sign up here to to become a eCoinSupply affiliate. Our Affiliate Team will review your application within two business days.

Once you have been accepted into the eCoinSupply affiliate program, you may begin displaying eCoinSupply Affiliate links on your site.

 About eCoinSupply and why should I join the affiliate program?

eCoinSupply offers both online and offline services. Our physical store, formerly known as Canada eCoinSupply ltd, has enabled eCoinSupply's unique and pioneer positioning within the crypto industry since 2017.

eCoinSupply is the trusted partner for safe and secure investments in crypto assets and serves more than 100,000 users from across Europe. eCoinSupply offers a premium service based on customer support and guidance, as well as shared knowledge through qualitative research and analysis on ICOs and underlying technologies behind crypto assets.

 What happens if a commission comes from a fraudulent signup?

A transaction is considered valid when a customer referred through an affiliate link has undertaken and concluded a transaction in accordance with payment instructions on the eCoinSupply platform.

eCoinSupply may disqualify Commissions in violation of the eCoinSupply agreement. Fore more information, please consult the Affiliate Agreement.

 What is the minimum payout amount?

The minimum payout amount is 100 $, paid in bitcoin.

 How are Affiliates paid? 

The Affiliate is remunerated 20% of the net revenues that have been generated from a referred customer throughout transactions undertaken on a one year span.

Commissions are paid in cryptocurrencies on a weekly basis. In order to be paid, the Affiliate must provide eCoinSupply with a valid BTC address.
