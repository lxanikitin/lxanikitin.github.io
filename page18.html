What is an eCoinPay Prepaid Card?

eCoinPay Prepaid card is a prepaid card you can top up instantly by converting funds in your eCoinSupply accounts to EUR, USD and GBP. It can be used anywhere regular prepaid cards are accepted.

You can order a Euro, US Dollar or British Pound prepaid card.

Plastic and virtual.

There are two types of eCoinPay prepaid cards: plastic and virtual.

Plastic cards are suitable for offline purchasing via POS and can also be used for ATM withdrawals. You can order a plastic eCoinPay card for €15/$15/£15 respectively.
Virtual cards are ideal for online shopping and account verifications. The price of a virtual eCoinPay card is €2.50/£2.50/$2.50 correspondingly.

Online or offline.

eCoinPay prepaid card works online, offline and internationally, making it simple for customers to use at millions of businesses around the world.

Go shopping easily.

It’s easy to convert the cryptocurrency in your eCoinSupply Bitcoin Wallet to EUR, USD or GBP and load onto your eCoinPay Prepaid Card and spend anywhere major cards are accepted.

Standard or Express delivery.

We always have cards in stock, so yours can be shipped the day after you place your order. The fastest option to receive it is a 3-10 working days DHL Express service.

How do I pay for a prepaid card?

You can pay for a prepaid card using any of the currencies accepted by eCoinSupply - Pounds, Dollars, Euro & Bitcoin. Once you’ve have sufficient funds, you will be able to order your card.

How do I activate my card?

Along with your prepaid card, you will receive instructions on how to activate your card. Please read them carefully. If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to contact our support.

How do I top up my eCoinPay Prepaid Card?

eCoinPay card is a prepaid card so you need to top it up first in order to be able to use it. Once you have funds in one of your eCoinSupply wallets, click Load on your Accounts page.

On the next page select a wallet to fund the card. You can top up your card from one of your Accounts (Euro, US dollars, Pound Sterling, Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin and XRP).

How do I pay for my eCoinPay card?

There are two ways you can pay for the card: using the balance of your eCoinSupply account or using a BTC transfer. We plan to add an option to pay with a bank card shortly.

To pay using the eCoinSupply wallet balance, please choose the account you would like to use.

If you've decided to go with Bitcoin transfer, you will need to send the required amount to a BTC address provided.

Please note: the invoice is only valid for a limited time (10 minutes max). You should pay as soon as possible after you order the card.
